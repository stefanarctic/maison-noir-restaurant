Meet the Master

Chef Pierre Dubois

Born in Lyon and trained under the greats of French gastronomy, Chef Dubois has spent three decades perfecting the art of seasonal, ingredient-driven cuisine. His philosophy: let nature lead, and technique follow.

Before Maison Noir, he earned his stars at L'Auberge du Pont de Collonges and Le Bernardin. Today, he leads our kitchen with humility and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

3 Michelin Stars • World's 50 Best • Meilleur Ouvrier de France
